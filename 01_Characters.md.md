
### **1. Base Description (All Tribes)**  
*"A highly evolved humanoid alien with subtle ant-like traits. Its body is sleek and human-proportioned, covered in a smooth, slightly glossy exoskeleton that resembles flexible armor. The face is nearly human but with faint chitinous texture along the jawline, large almond-shaped eyes with a faint insect-like shimmer, and short, delicate antennas curling just above the brow. The hands have four fingers and an opposable thumb, tipped with small claws. The creature stands tall with an elegant, athletic posture."*  

**Variations by Tribe:**  

### **2. Crimson Tribe (Red Hues)**  
*"A warrior-like humanoid ant-alien with deep crimson and burgundy exoskeleton, its surface gleaming like polished ruby under light. The eyes are a striking gold-amber, glowing faintly. Tribal markings in black or darker red trace along its arms and back, resembling intricate scar patterns. The short antennas twitch with alertness, their tips slightly darker. The face is almost human but with a sharp, regal bone structure and faint mandible outlines near the mouth."*  

### **3. Azure Tribe (Blue Hues)**  
*"A graceful, aquatic-toned ant-humanoid with a sleek exoskeleton in shades of cerulean, sapphire, and teal, shifting like liquid metal in the light. Its large, pupil-less eyes are silver or pale blue, reflecting light like mirrors. Delicate bioluminescent patterns run along its collarbone and limbs, pulsing softly. The antennas are thin and translucent, faintly glowing. The face is serene, almost elven, with only a subtle insectoid sharpness to the cheekbones and jaw."*  

### **4. Verdant Tribe (Green Hues)**  
*"A nimble, forest-dwelling ant-alien with an exoskeleton in emerald, moss, and jade tones, textured like polished beetle shell. Its eyes are large and luminous, in shades of gold or deep green. Vine-like tribal tattoos wrap around its limbs, glowing faintly in dim light. The antennas are short and flexible, flicking like a cat’s whiskers. The face is the most human-like, with only a faint chitinous ridge along the nose and brow, giving it an otherworldly elegance."*
