Character creation:  
"Create a character for a [genre] story. Name: [optional]. Role: [protagonist/antagonist/ally]. Core desire: [what they want]. Core fear: [what they avoid]. One secret: [something they hide]. One habit: [distinctive behavior]."

Setting description:  
"Describe [location name] in [time period/weather]. Include: what it looks like, smells like, sounds like, and the mood it creates. Write 3-5 sentences."

Dialogue coaching:  
"Here is a scene: [brief scenario]. Write 8 lines of dialogue between [character A] and [character B]. Character A is [trait]. Character B is [trait]. Goal of the scene: [what advances the plot]."
